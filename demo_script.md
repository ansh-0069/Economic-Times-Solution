# FinMentor AI — 4-Minute Demo Script

**Total time: 4:00 | Pace: Conversational, not rushed**

---

## ACT 1: The Problem (0:00 – 0:40)

> "India has 14 crore mutual fund investors. Most of them get a CAMS statement every month — a dense 20-page PDF full of NAVs, folios, and transaction codes. Nobody reads it. And that's costing them lakhs."
>
> "Meet Rahul. He's 32, earns 1.2 lakh a month, invests in 5 mutual funds. He thinks he's doing great. But his funds overlap by 55%, he's bleeding 15,000 rupees a year in hidden expense ratios, and he's nowhere close to his retirement goal."
>
> "Rahul doesn't need another stock-tip app. He needs a financial X-ray. That's what we built."

---

## ACT 2: The Upload — 10 Seconds to Truth (0:40 – 1:10)

**[Screen: FinMentor AI Upload Page]**

> "This is FinMentor AI. You upload your CAMS PDF — that's it. One file. No forms, no questionnaires."

**[Click 'Try Demo Analysis' or upload the sample PDF]**

> "Behind the scenes, three things happen simultaneously:
> 1. **pdfplumber** extracts every transaction from the PDF — no OCR, no guessing.
> 2. A **deterministic math engine** — not the LLM — calculates your true XIRR using pyxirr, computes overlap matrices, and runs a 10-year expense drag projection.
> 3. **Gemini 2.5 Flash** synthesizes the numbers into a human verdict — but it never does the math. That's the key design choice. LLMs hallucinate numbers. We don't let them."

## Pro Tips for Delivery

- **Start the backend and frontend BEFORE the demo.** Have the upload page ready.
- **Pre-upload the PDF once** so the analysis is cached. Then "upload" live for the demo — it'll be instant.
- **Keep the Telegram bot running** in a separate tab. Switch to it seamlessly.
- **The voice input is your showstopper.** Practice the mic button — speak clearly, pause, let it transcribe. Judges will remember this.
- **End on the impact number.** "850 crore in hidden fees" — let it land.
